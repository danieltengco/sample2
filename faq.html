Frequently Asked Questions
  • How do I pay for my movie ticket?
    We are currently accepting only credit card payments.

  • I need to purchase more than 6 tickets. What should I do?
    If you wish to buy more than 6 tickets, please contact us.

  • Do I need to bring a printed copy of my ticket?
    No, you may simply save a copy of the QR code on your mobile device and show it to the cinema attendant upon entry.

  • May I refund my ticket?
    All sales are final and cannot be refunded.

  • Is bringing of food allowed?
    Yes, snacks purchased at our movie snack bar may be brought inside the cinema.
